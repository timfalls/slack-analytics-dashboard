# slack-analytics-dashboard
A browser-based tool for turning Slack Analytics exports into formatted weekly reports. 
No install, no login, no data leaves your browser.

Upload your Members CSV and Channel Stats CSV from Slack Analytics, fill in any gaps manually, and the tool generates two outputs: a formatted weekly update ready to paste into Slack, and a tab-separated row ready to paste into a spreadsheet tracker. 

Remembers last week's member count to auto-calculate week-over-week delta. Keeps a running history of previous weeks.
